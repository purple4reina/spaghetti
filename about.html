About

Hi. I'm Rey. When everyone else was panic buying toilet paper in March 2020, I panic bought pasta. All that was left on the shelf was spaghetti. I ended up with 20 pounds of it.

Since there's only so much spaghetti with marinara sauce one can take, I started to branch out. Never one to go with the flow, I decided to go a more creative route with my creations.
